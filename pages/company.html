About Us

An International Presence & Strong Track Record

Phoenix Evolution DMCC is the exclusive MENA partner of Engage Hub, a leading global technology provider of cross-channel engagement solutions. Phoenix Evolution's local team bring our regional customers a potent mix of global experience allied with local market knowledge.

Today, we're operating in 20 different countries and our cloud technology is processing over 3.8 billion interactions per year — delivering highly personalized, connected experiences across multiple channels to over 556 million customers around the world.

Headquartered in Dubai with supporting offices throughout Europe, Asia and South America, we're better connected than ever before.
