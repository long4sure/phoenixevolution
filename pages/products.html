Cross Channel Messaging

Communicate with customers at the right time, on the right channel, in a seamless and personalized way.

WhatsApp is now a primary communication channel for consumers globally. Our platform enables businesses to deliver two-way, personalized conversations via WhatsApp — from notifications and alerts to full customer support journeys — at scale.
WeChat is the dominant messaging platform in China with over 1 billion active users. Our integration allows businesses to reach Chinese customers and communities on the channel they use most, enabling seamless support and marketing campaigns.
SMS and MMS remain among the most effective channels for reaching customers — with open rates exceeding 90%. Deliver time-sensitive alerts, promotions, and rich media content directly to any mobile device, instantly.
